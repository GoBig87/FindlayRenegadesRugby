How to use Fin

Locate. Scan. Unlock.

  • Locate a Trailer

    From inside the Fin app, locate a trailer map icon. The circle above the map icon will alert you to how many boards are currently on the trailer. Tapping the map icon allows you to view the inventory of what type of boards are currently attached.

  • Scan Padlock Code

    Locate the padlock on that is attached to the board you wish to rent. On the padlock there will be a QR code and a 4 digit code. Manually enter the code or scan it with your camera.

  • Unlock Padlock

    Follow the on-screen instructions inside the Fin app to unlock the padlock. Untether the cable from the board and paddle and start your rental.

  • Ending Rental

    To end the rental repeat the unlock process and attach the board back onto the trailer and padlock.

High quality boards for a low cost

Fin offers high quality epoxy boards. Paddle boards come in 10 ft and 12 ft. Paddle boards come with a life jacket, paddle and surf leash. Surfboards come in 6 ft and 8 ft fun boards and also include a surf leash.

  • San Diego Rentals

    Fin currently only operates in the San Diego Mission Bay area but is looking to expand soon!

  • Record Your Ride

    All boards have embedded GPS tracking devices inside them. At the end of your rental you can choose to link the GPS data to your rental to get a map of your ride.

  • Multiple Rentals

    IOS users are able to rent multiple boards at once. Fin keeps track of each rental separately to easily split up between friends.

  • Friend Referral

    Got a friend who would love Fin? Send them a referral from the Fin app and get a free rental when you both have at least one rental in your rental history.
